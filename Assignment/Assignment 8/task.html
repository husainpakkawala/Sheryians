7 Wonders Of The World

Taj Mahal

A symbol of eternal love built by Emperor Shah Jahan in white marble.

Great Wall of China

An ancient defense wall stretching over mountains for thousands of miles.

Christ the Redeemer

A towering statue of Jesus overlooking Rio de Janeiro with open arms.

Colosseum

Rome’s grand ancient amphitheatre once used for gladiator battles.

Machu Picchu

A hidden Incan city set high in the Andes, surrounded by misty peaks.

Chichen Itza

A Mayan pyramid famous for its precise astronomy and architecture.

Petra

An ancient city carved into red sandstone cliffs, known as the “Rose City.”
